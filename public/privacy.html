Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Pavio app for mobile devices and web browsers, together with any related services operated by Pavio (collectively, the "Application"). Pavio is referred to as the "Service Provider".

Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your user ID assigned by the system
  • Your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Purchase history and subscription information

How We Use Your Information

The Service Provider uses the information collected to:

  • Provide and improve the Application
  • Process payments and subscriptions

  • Provide customer support
  • Communicate important updates

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Razorpay (payment processing)
  • Supabase (data storage)

Data Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting your data. All data is transmitted using SSL/HTTPS encryption. Your information is stored securely on Supabase servers.

Data Retention

The Service Provider will retain user-provided data for as long as you use the Application and for a reasonable time thereafter.

Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at support@pavio.tech. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The Application is intended for users aged 18 and above. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect information from children under 18.
